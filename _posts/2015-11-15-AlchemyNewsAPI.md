With Alchemy News API, you can get news via web easily.
To use API, you need to get APP key first.

<span style="font-size:10px;"><a href="http://www.igosso.net/flk/8573457815.html" target="_blank"><img src="https://farm9.staticflickr.com/8112/8573457815_af9df726db_m.jpg" alt="" /></a><br />Yesterdays news / (Mick Baker)rooster</span>


Here's sample code.
I try to get news about "Google".


~~~~
$APPKEY="YOUR APP KEY";
$KEYWORD="Google";//put your word


$url = "https://access.alchemyapi.com/calls/data/GetNews?outputMode=json&amp;start=now-1d&amp;end=now&amp;count=5&amp;".
"q.enriched.url.enrichedTitle.keywords.keyword.text=".$KEYWORD."&amp;return=enriched.url.url,enriched.url.title&amp;apikey=".$APPKEY;


$curl = curl_init();
curl_setopt($curl, CURLOPT_POST, true);
curl_setopt($curl, CURLOPT_URL, $url);
curl_setopt($curl, CURLOPT_RETURNTRANSFER, true);
curl_setopt($curl, CURLOPT_SAFE_UPLOAD, true);
curl_setopt($curl, CURLOPT_SSL_VERIFYPEER, false);
$result = curl_exec($curl);
curl_close($curl);
print $result;
~~~~

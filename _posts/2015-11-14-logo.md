With Clearbit's company logo API, you can show company logo images easily. 
No need to get images from companies. 
To use this API, just set a src of image file to "https://logo.clearbit.com/{company URL}"



For more information, visit the site below.

[Clearbit](http://blog.clearbit.com/)
